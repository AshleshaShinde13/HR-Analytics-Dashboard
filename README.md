**PROJECT OVERVIEW**

The project features an HR Analytics Dashboard built using Power BI to analyze workforce demographics, compensation, and hiring trends. The dashboard provides interactive insights into 
employee headcount, job roles, gender and age distribution, salary vs qualification, and year-over-year growth. It helps HR teams and stakeholders make data-driven workforce planning and 
diversity decisions through clear, executive-ready visualizations.

**OBJECTIVES**

- Visualize total employee headcount, average salary, and leave balance KPIs
- Analyze workforce distribution by job role, gender, and age group
- Identify salary patterns across qualification levels
- Track year-over-year employee growth and hiring trends
- Enable interactive filtering by job title, gender, age, and year

CONCLUSION

This HR dashboard effectively converts employee data into clear, actionable workforce insights. It enables HR teams and leadership to monitor headcount trends, understand
